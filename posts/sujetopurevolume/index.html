Tribute to PureVolume

Check out the GitHub repository and the website.

PureVolume was a website for the discovery and promotion of new music and emerging artists. The mission was to give artists a new promotion tool. The site shut down on June 30, 2018. Luckily it was archived by Wayback Machine.

This is a profile page of a band. For the music player is used this one by Zakari Abdessamad.
